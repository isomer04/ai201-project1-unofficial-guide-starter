Google Software Engineer Interview Experiences (Glassdoor)

Candidate 1 - No offer, Positive experience, Average interview
Interviewed at Google, Warsaw.

Remote round: on the same day, 45-minute technical interview then 45-minute behavioral interview. Plenty of time to ask questions afterward - be prepared with at least three questions to show interest.

Question: Given a list of ranges like [0,1], [2,5], ... create a class with methods to find if an element is in any range and to insert a new range.
