Apple Interview Experiences (Blind)

Apple Recruiter Call after Final Interview
Recruiter emailed 24 hours after final interview to schedule a 10-minute call. 10 minutes is not a lot of time - hard to tell if this is good or bad. US employment applications arrived 3 hours after final interview, then in email she asked about AI. The wait is very stressful.

Rejected at Apple phone screen
I had an Apple phone screen with a Director (Head of Technical Leadership) for a Full Stack Engineer role. He asked me three questions:
1. What was your most challenging project?
2. What AI tools are you using at work?
3. What is your current role at your company?

I answered everything clearly but still got rejected.

Apple onsite rejection - what could have gone wrong?
Interviewed with Apple Ads for ICT4. Process was: first round coding interview, second round coding and system design, and then an in-person onsite with coding, system design, and HM round.

I genuinely felt like I nailed the onsite. Coding and system design felt straightforward, interviewers seemed engaged. Got rejected.

Apple interviews - are they really hiring?
Got reached out by a persistent recruiter at Apple for a role. Recruiter kept pushing and saying the team wanted to meet the next day. Apple's interview interest does not always signal active headcount.

Apple recruiter call after final loop
Recruiter sent an email 2 days after final loop with Apple's hardware team saying she had an update to share and wanted to schedule a phone call.

Failed OpenAI interview, landed Airbnb and Apple offers
Apple offer came in close to 750k TC and matched Airbnb's offer. Apple matched competitor offers during negotiation.

Apple HM phone screen - response timeline
Candidates report either being notified quickly (same day) or being ghosted completely. Decision after screen can be very quick - within a few hours. No response after 2-3 business days often signals no hire.

Finished two phone interviews at Apple, hiring manager sent LinkedIn connection request after a couple of hours
Had two phone screening interviews at Apple and the hiring manager (who did not interview me) sent a LinkedIn connection request shortly after. No recruiter update for several days.

Apple Onsite Rejection
Recently failed Apple onsite without much preparation. Recruiter said the HM asked to share CV with other teams - this can indicate a positive impression even without an offer from the original team.

Apple interview results timeline
Decision after screen was very quick - within a few hours. Longer waits after full panel - no response after 2-3 business days may mean no hire, but timelines vary.
