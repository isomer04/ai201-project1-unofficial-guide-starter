Candidate 1 - No offer, Neutral experience, Difficult interview
Interviewed at Amazon, Apr 2025. Process took 5 months.

Technical assessment into a work life simulation, then final loop interview (3 consecutive interviews, 2 technical then 1 behavioral). Scheduling issues delayed the final loop for over four months.

Question: Design an employee animal daycare system.
