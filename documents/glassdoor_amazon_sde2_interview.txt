Amazon Software Development Engineer II Interview Experiences (Glassdoor)

Candidate 1 - Declined offer, Positive experience, Average interview
Interviewed at Amazon, Seattle, WA, May 2026.

Two rounds total. First round with an SDE II: Data Structures and Algorithms, LeetCode-style questions. Second round with an SDM: Behavioral and System Design - Leadership Principles and past work experience impact.

Candidate 2 - No offer, Negative experience, Easy interview
Interviewed at Amazon, Feb 2026.

Recruiter explicitly mentioned that the Technical Phone Screen would not contain any behavioral questions, but the interviewer asked 3 behavioral questions. The coding problem was a pretty trivial DFS problem, not appropriate for an SDE 2 role or useful to demonstrate candidate skills.

Note from candidate: They care a lot more about what you've accomplished than whatever you can accomplish based on your skills.

Questions asked:
1. Tell me about a time when you had to meet a tight deadline.
2. Tell me about a time when you had to do more than what you were required to do.
3. Tell me about a time where you made a significant impact on a customer.
